Overview:


I have been a hobbyist woodworker for many years. Most of my recent projects have been done on a wood-lathe, but I have also used other tools to make such things as wooden practice weapons for aikido, small boxes, and even furniture.

I own a mini wood-lathe, which I first got for the purpose of making my own replacement violin pegs for an old violin that I bought at an estate sale. I have used it more recently to produce wooden sound chambers for custom-built headphones. However, that particular task is better suited to a full-sized wood-lathe, which I have been fortunate enough to have access to occasionally.
