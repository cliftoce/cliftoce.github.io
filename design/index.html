Overview:


I occasionally create T-shirt designs to enter in the weekly Shirt.Woot! Derby. I became especially involved with this during the summer of 2011 and have continued to produce designs periodically since then.

Check out the gallery of my shirt designs.

Most of my graphic design is done in Photoshop. I have also experimented with simple 3D modeling and animation in Blender, as well as vector graphics in Adobe Illustrator.
