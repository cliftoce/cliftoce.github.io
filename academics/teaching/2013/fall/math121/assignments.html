EC #4 Extra Credit #4: "Online Gateway," worth 5 points.
  • Pass the online Gateway exam by 11:59 p.m. on 09/27/13! It often takes a few attempts to become familiar with the format of the exam, so don't wait until the last minute. The Gateway is timed at 32 minutes per attempt, and you must answer a minimum of 8/10 questions correctly in order to pass. There is no limit on the number of retakes, so you can cancel an attempt at any time without penalty. The emphasis of the Gateway is to test your knowledge of Differentiation Rules (Sections 3.1 - 3.7); for each question, simply compute the derivative of the given function. It is not necessary to simplify your answers, but be careful to use the correct formatting and symbols when typing them in. Use the Preview button to check that you have typed in each solution the way you intended! For more details, read the FAQ and Gateway: Tips and Tricks.
