Outreach:


Every April is Mathematics Awareness Month (MAM). From 2012 through 2015, I organized and assisted with competitions and activities for K-12 students to raise mathematics awareness.

As a member of the IEEE CSS Technical Committeee on Control Education, the AACC Technical Committee on Education, and the IFAC Technical Committee 9.4 on Control Education, I also assisted with the recurring "Beauty of Controls" workshop for high school students and teachers.





Events:


April 15 and April 22, 2015.
Coordinated the "Students + Workshops = Success + Fun" workshops for 5th grade students from Broken Arrow Elementary School (April 15) and Cordley Elementary School (April 22), held in conjunction with Mathematics Awareness Month, Lawrence, KS.

December 15, 2014.
Asssisted with "The Power, Beauty and Excitement of the Cross-Boundaries Nature of Control, a Field that Spans Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM)" workshop for high school students and teachers, held in conjunction with the 53rd IEEE Conference on Decision and Control, Los Angeles, CA.

August 29, 2014.
