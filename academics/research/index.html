Research:


My doctoral research at The University of Kansas Department of Mathematics, under the supervision of Professor Bozenna Pasik-Duncan, focused on parameter estimation and adaptive control techniques applied to a stochastic system model for PageRank.

For my undergraduate senior thesis in Mathematics at Whitman College, I studied the commutativity of elements in non-abelian groups and published a note on this subject with Professors David Guichard and Patrick Keef.





Interest Areas:


  • PageRank algorithm, stochastic systems, parameter estimation and adaptive control.
