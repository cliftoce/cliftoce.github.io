Education:


My early education involved many years of homeschooling. I began taking courses at Clackamas Community College at a young age and eventually transferred credits to Whitman College where I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics. I then earned Master's and Ph.D. degrees in Mathematics at The University of Kansas under the supervision of Professor Bozenna Pasik-Duncan.
