Overview:


I have been practicing the Japanese martial art of Aikido since August 1999. I presently hold the rank of Nidan, second degree black belt.

I am a student of Craig Fife Sensei, the founder and chief instructor of Aikido Northwest in Milwaukie, OR. Aikido Northwest follows the system and curriculum of the Aikikai Hombu Dojo (the world headquarters of the Aikikai Foundation) in Tokyo, Japan. Within the US, Aikido Northwest is an affiliated dojo of the California Aikido Association (CAA). The CAA is is structured in three divisions, each comprised of dojos throughout the United States and countries abroad. Aikido Northwest is part of Division II, headed by Frank Doran Shihan.

I have also spent significant time training at four other locations over the years:

2010 - 2015.
I served as an instructor (as well as President for one year and VP for two years) of the KU Ki-Aikido Club at The University of Kansas. I also trained at the club's affiliated Shin Shin Toitsu Aikido dojo, the Kansas Ki Society, in Lawrence, KS.

2008 - 2010.
I founded and served as chief instructor of the Whitman Aikido Club at Whitman College in Walla Walla, WA.

2006.
I trained with a Yoshinkan Aikido group that met at the Kickstart Fitness Centre in Townsville, Australia.

2001 - 2002.
I trained at Guam Aikikai on the US island territory of Guam.
