Overview:


I have been practicing the Japanese martial art of Aikido since August 1999 (discounting a few-month stint in 1996 that I can hardly remember). My home dojo is Aikido Northwest in Milwaukie, OR.

During two years at Whitman College, I founded and served as instructor for the first Whitman Aikido Club.

During five years at The University of Kansas, I served as an instructor (as well as President for one year and VP for two years) for the KU Ki-Aikido Club. I also trained at the affiliated Kansas Ki Society dojo in Lawrence, KS.
