Instruments:


I primarily play mandolin, violin/fiddle, and banjo (both 5-string and Irish tenor).

My current collection of instruments is as follows.





Mandolin:


  • Eastman MD505.
    • Woods: spruce top, maple back/sides/neck, and radiused ebony fingerboard.
    • Dimensions: 13.88" scale length (23-fret) and 1.09" nut width.
             

  • Washburn M1S (old/travel mandolin).
    • Woods: spruce top, maple back/sides/neck, and flat rosewood fingerboard.
    • Dimensions: 13.75" scale length (20-fret) and 1.13" nut width.
             





Violin:


  • Gerardt 300.
             

  • Unnamed early 1900's German-made violin.
             





Banjo:


  • Gold Tone TS-250 (tenor).
    • Woods: maple neck/rim, rosewood fingerboard, and mahogany resonator.
    • Dimensions: 22.63" scale length (19-fret), 1.19" nut width, 11" head, and 14" resonator.
             

  • Gold Tone BG-250F (5-string).
    • Woods: maple neck/rim, rosewood fingerboard, and mahogany resonator.
    • Dimensions: 26.19" scale length (22-fret), 1.19" nut width, 11" head, and 14" resonator.
