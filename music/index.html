Overview:


Beginning in 2002, I took private violin lessons and played in an orchestra. In 2007, I transitioned to playing in the Clackamas Community College Chamber Ensemble. Also in 2007, I sponteneously decided to learn the mandolin, so I picked up a cheap one and began to teach myself; however, it wasn't until a few years later that I really got into it. I started learning the 5-string banjo in 2012, followed by the Irish tenor banjo in 2014.

These days, the mandolin is my go-to instrument, but I still bring out the others from time to time. I especially enjoy Celtic music, but also play some classical and bluegrass.
